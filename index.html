About My Work My Journey

Hi! I'm Will.

I create products that have an impact. I am excited by my projects, my passions, and the people that surround me.

Who I Am

I am an incoming Senior at Washington University in St. Louis studying Computer Science and Entrepreneurship, with a minor in Human and Computer Interaction. I was born and raised in Connecticut, where I fell in love with technology and gaming.

Developing my first Minecraft mod introduced me to the power of creating with code, and I fell in love. I enjoy working on all aspects of product development, from defining the function of a product and mastering the design, to shipping code and iterating off feedback.

Along the way, I picked up business strategy skills through startups that I created with my friends and family. I have met some inspiring mentors and love hearing about people’s journey through their past, present, and future.

I thrive in team environments and highly value getting other people's suggestions.

I care about the how and the why of what I build.

What I Do

I lead teams and build products that have a purpose. I create cool companies with my friends and I am constantly excited by the work I do.

What I've Done

I've developed mobile apps while overseeing large teams of engineers. I've been a commissioner of an esports league and a producer for a lifestyle TV show. I have founded two companies in technology and media, respectively.

What I Love

My tastes in music, television, and, most importantly, video games define who I am. When I am not coding, you can catch me DJing the latest Illenium song, watching The Mandalorian, or competing as the captain of my school’s VALORANT team.

I love being outdoors, usually in the form of mountain biking or skiing (depending on the season). I also play volleyball and will always dive for a dig.

What's Next

This summer, I am an Associate Product Manager Intern at Salesforce, building products to help marketers reach their target audience. On the side, I am building a few side projects focused on empowering gamers and creators.

I plan to pursue a career in product management. I am a life-long entrepreneur and will continue to pursue ventures that fuel my passion and evoke change.

I love to meet new people, feel free to reach out!
