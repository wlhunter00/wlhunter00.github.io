Who I Am

I am an incoming Junior at Washington University in St. Louis studying Computer Science and Entreprenurship, with a minor in Human and Computer Interaction. I was born and raised in Connecticut, where I fell in love with computing.


Ever since I developed my first Minecraft mod, I knew the world of technology was where I belonged. I enjoy working on all aspects of development, from defining the function of a product and mastering the design, to experiencing the joy of finally getting my code to compile.


Along the way, I picked up business strategy skills through startups that I created with my friends and family. I have met some inspiring mentors and love hearing about people’s journey through their past, present, and future.


I thrive in team environments and care deeply about the how and the why of what I build.
