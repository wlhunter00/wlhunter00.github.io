Practicum

  • May 2020

  • Co-Founder

  • Personal Venture

Problem

Students can only work in an agile environment through an internship, but COVID-19 canceled many of these opportunities. Without the formal structure of a company, it is difficult for CS students to work on a meaningful project.

Product

Practicum is a program designed to place students on teams that operate as a startup. The projects are sourced from entrepreneurs that need help developing an MVP. Teams have mentors that can guide the students through development.

Process

  • Developed a recruitment pipeline, which generated 300+ applications and 15+ clients in two weeks. Interviewed students to find pain points in our processes.
  • Lead a team of 8 partners to build the curriculum. The primary KPI was conversion rate between interview rounds, which averaged 88%.
  • Vetted potential clients to ensure quality, maintaining a 33% acceptance rate. Flushed out features of products for student development teams.

React Native Express Product Management

Project Covid

  • March 2020

  • Co-Founder

  • Head of Product

  • LFR International

Problem

Information online regarding COVID-19 today is far too scattered and complicated for the average person to understand. There is also a growing concern over the spread of misinformation and “fake news” on the topic.

Product

Project Covid is a mobile app that aggregates vetted information regarding the pandemic. The app includes statistics, the latest news, educational toolkits, and local testing information and presents all sources used by the team.

Process

  • Wrote 50+ user stories and prioritized which features the team should pursue, met with stakeholders and users to understand the use cases for the app.
  • Managed a team of 12 engineers, assigning user stories based on experience and helped resolve roadblocks. Wrote majority of Express server used by app.
  • Deployed backend to Heroku server and oversaw launch of the app on TestFlight. Received feedback from 80+ beta testers and overhauled the home screen.
